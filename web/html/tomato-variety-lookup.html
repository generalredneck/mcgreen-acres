Black Angel
Slicer 		Dark Crimson Early producer of clean, 6–10 oz. lightly ribbed fruits with excellent flavor. Compact, high-yielding plants are easy to trellis for tunnel or field. Dark interior even at an early stage due to the crimson gene. A striking addition to any market display alongside red varieties. Determinate. Johnny's
Black Cherry
Cherry 		Purple-Brown Sweet and robust heirloom bred in Florida by the late Vince Sapp. Round, 20 gm. fruits are a pleasant purple-brown color, regarded as one of the best-tasting black cherry tomatoes available. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Bronze Torch
Specialty 		Brick Red / Bronze Stripe Delicious small saladette in the Juliet size class. Fruits average 1½ oz. in a beautiful shade of brick red with bronze stripes. Prolific early producer, crack-resistant, and performs well under heavy disease pressure. A dramatic offering at market, great for specialty mixes. Earlier and more productive than Pink Tiger. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Candy Bell
Grape 		Red A labor-saving determinate grape tomato with great taste and a manageable plant. Maintains good fruit size and consistent production. Ideal for growers who want a sweet, prolific grape tomato without the sprawl of indeterminate types. Determinate. Johnny's
Carbon
Heirloom 		Black / Deep Purple Delicious, highly productive black heirloom. Resists cracking and cat-facing better than other large black heirlooms, producing a higher proportion of market-quality fruit. Rich, complex flavor with a sweet finish. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Cherry Bomb
Cherry 		Red Late blight-resistant field cherry, ideal for organic growers who need strong late blight protection. Vigorous plants produce high yields of well-flavored red cherry tomatoes. A reliable workhorse for field production. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Citrine
Cherry 		Tangerine Orange Johnny's-bred tangerine cherry developed to solve Sun Gold's cracking problem. Exceptional snacker with rich, balanced flavor, a meaty bite, and strong resistance to splitting. Ideal cherry size at 20 gm. Vigorous plant selected for low-input systems under heavy disease pressure. Developed in collaboration with NC State University. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Clementine
Cocktail 		Orange Johnny's-bred orange cocktail tomato with a unique sweet-tart flavor derived from the Beta gene — the same orange trait found in Sun Gold. Polite, open plant habit fits well in close quarters and ventilates well. Pairs beautifully with Mountain Magic. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Defiant PhR
Slicer 		Red Bred for earliness, disease resistance, and flavor. This mid-size slicer has high resistance to late blight (Ph resistance) and delivers great flavor even under heavy disease pressure. A dependable go-to for organic and conventional growers in blight-prone regions. Determinate. Johnny's
Estiva
Slicer 		Deep Red Bred in the Mediterranean region for low-tech tunnel production, Estiva produces remarkably uniform 7–9 oz. fruits that resist cracking. Known for an uncanny ability to set fruit in excessive heat, with fully loaded trusses over a long season. Outstanding flavor and texture. Well balanced for high tunnels and field. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Galahad
Slicer 		Red Fantastic flavor with an impressive disease resistance profile covering regions from the Midwest to the Deep South. Round, 7–12 oz. fruits can be harvested with green shoulders and ripened in storage, or ripened on the vine. Resistance to early blight makes it a strong contender in the North. AAS Regional Winner (Heartland, West/Northwest). Determinate. Johnny's
Glacier
Heirloom 		Red Tasty first-early heirloom variety. Cocktail-sized 1–2 oz. red fruits borne on a compact determinate plant. Sweetness combines with moderate acidity to create a well-balanced flavor. A classic for short-season growing. Determinate. Johnny's

 Ghost Cherry
Cherry 		Ivory / Cream Prolific heirloom white cherry tomato producing high yields of 2–3 oz. ivory-yellow fruits with a slightly fuzzy, soft skin. Excellent sweet flavor. Striking visual contrast in a mixed harvest basket. Indeterminate. (Not currently listed at Johnny's — general description.) Not at Johnny's
Granadero
Paste 		Bright Red Very high yields of uniform, attractive, bright red 4–5 oz. plum tomatoes with excellent flavor. Thick-walled fruits ideal for fresh tomato sauces, salsas, and salads. Broad disease resistance keeps Granadero healthy even under heavy disease pressure. Low susceptibility to blossom end rot. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Jolene
Slicer 		Deep Red Excels in the Southern U.S. Smooth, deep red beefsteak that sets fruit in heat when other tomatoes will not. Round, clean, 8–10 oz. lightly ribbed fruits. An exceptional choice for Texas and Gulf Coast growers dealing with extended summer heat. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Juliet
Paste 		Deep Red Great flavor fresh or cooked. One of the most disease-resistant varieties in Johnny's trials. Deep red, shiny, small plum-shaped fruits averaging 1–2 oz. in large clusters. Prolific producer with exceptional shelf life. An AAS Award winner and perennial grower favorite. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Magic Bullet
Specialty 		Green / Yellow / Pink / Indigo Very productive and tasty multicolored snacking tomato with dark indigo shoulders over green, yellow, and pink base colors. Striking visual appeal at market. A head-turning Indigo-type variety with improved plant habit. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Marmalade Skies
Specialty 		Orange Orange tomato with late blight resistance. Clean, blemish-free fruits ripen uniformly with no green shoulder. A standout at farm stands for its vibrant color and consistent appearance. Indeterminate. Johnny's
🍅
 Midnight Roma
Paste 		Deep Purple / Black Dark purple-black Roma-type developed at Oregon State University by crossing Indigo Rose and Oregon Star. Rich in anthocyanins — the same antioxidant pigments found in blueberries. Thick-walled paste tomato with surprisingly intense flavor for the type. Excellent for sauces and striking on a market table. Indeterminate. (OSU variety; not currently listed at Johnny's.) OSU / Not at Johnny's
Mimosa
Grape 		Orange Orange grape tomato with fruity flavor, leaf blight resistance, and a long harvest window. A Johnny's-developed variety. Eye-catching color and sweet flavor make it a favorite for snacking and market display. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Moskvich
Heirloom 		Deep Red One of the most appealing extra-early heirloom tomatoes. Early, deep red, and cold-tolerant fruits with rich flavor. Smooth and globe-shaped, 4–6 oz., with a small stem scar. A Russian heirloom well suited for short growing seasons and early market production. Indeterminate. Johnny's

 Ox Heart
Heirloom 		Pink-Red Classic large heirloom oxheart-type tomato with a distinctive heart-shaped profile. Meaty flesh, few seeds, and low gel content make it excellent for slicing, sauces, and canning. Rich, full flavor uncommon in modern hybrids. Fruits typically 8–16 oz. Indeterminate. (Heirloom type; not currently listed at Johnny's — general description.) Heirloom / Not at Johnny's

 Ozark Pink
Heirloom 		Pink Heat-tolerant Southern heirloom producing large, meaty pink beefsteak fruits with excellent flavor. Developed for reliability in hot, humid climates like the Ozark region. Sweet, low-acid flesh. A strong performer in Texas summers. Indeterminate. (Not currently listed at Johnny's — general description.) Not at Johnny's
Red Pearl
Grape 		Red Tender and nearly seedless grape tomato with intermediate resistance to late blight. Fruits weigh 15–20 gm. with good flavor and meaty texture. A clean, blemish-free market grape tomato with reliable production. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Sun Gold
Cherry 		Golden Orange The gold standard of orange cherry tomatoes — introduced by Johnny's to the U.S. in 1991. Delectably sweet and fruity with a tropical flavor no other cherry matches. Prolific producer of small orange-gold fruits. Note: ripe fruits tend to crack and drop if not harvested promptly. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Sunpeach
Cherry 		Deep Pink Delicious pink cherry tomato with leaf mold resistance. A loyal customer favorite at Johnny's for its productivity, crack resistance, and incredible flavor. Deep pink, shiny, 15–20 gm. cherries borne on long trusses. Vigorous, healthy, high-yielding plants. Indeterminate. Johnny's
Supersweet 100
Cherry 		Red The classic sweet red cherry tomato. Reliable producer with prolific yields of great-tasting, 15–20 gm. fruits produced in large clusters. Widely adapted and consistently popular with both growers and customers. A proven market standard. Indeterminate. Johnny's
